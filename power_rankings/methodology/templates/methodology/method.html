Ranking methodology

This data is based on esports data provided by riot games located here: Link to data

This is over 500 Gigabytes of data about esports games from the past 4 years

Some regions have more teams with data than others such as NA and EMEA

Stats are collecter for each player and for each team, scaled between 1 and 100, and multiplied with weights to obtain skill

Stats are scaled using the following formula:X \[ X_{\text{scaled}} = \frac{X - X_{\text{min}}}{X_{\text{max}} - X_{\text{min}}}\]

Player skill: player wins, player losses, kda, turrets destroyed,first bloods

Team skill: team winrate, divided into four catagories by year game played, region,avgPlayerSkill,level

Region skill: international winrate, international representation/games played

The weight values are arbitrary and chosen by the developer of this website based on trial and error

  • ancientWeight = .01
  • oldWeight = .02
  • agedWeight = .07
  • youngWeight = .2
  • levelWeight = .25
  • regionWeight = .2
  • playerWeight = .25
